Lose You To Love Me

"Lose You to Love Me" is a poignant ballad that showcases Selena Gomez's raw vulnerability and emotional depth.
Through hauntingly beautiful vocals and evocative lyrics, Gomez takes listeners on a journey of self-discovery and healing.
The song reflects on the pain of letting go of a toxic relationship, but also celebrates the empowerment that comes from choosing self-love and moving forward.
