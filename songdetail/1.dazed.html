Dazed & Confused

"Dazed & Confused" marks the third single of 2015 for the Canadian musician Ruel, following "Don’t Tell Me" and "Younger."
In this track, Ruel navigates the tumultuous feelings of infatuation and uncertainty that often accompany young love.
With heartfelt lyrics and a soulful voice, he captures the highs and lows of emotional confusion, hoping to find clarity and connection amidst the chaos.
