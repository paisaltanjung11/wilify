If The World Was Ending

“If The World Was Ending” constitutes the third single of 2019 for the Canadian musician JP
Saxe, following “Same Room” and “Women Who Look Like You”.
Throughout the song, Jonathan, together with Julia Michaels, reminisces over a past and
flawed relationship, hopefully it can start again with the best intentions and a good
amount of patience.
