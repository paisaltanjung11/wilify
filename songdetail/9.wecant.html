we can’t be friends (wait for your love)

"We Can't Be Friends (Wait for Your Love)" by Ariana Grande is a soulful ballad that explores the complexities of unrequited love and the struggle to move on from a past relationship.
Grande's emotive vocals and heartfelt lyrics convey the pain of longing for someone who can't reciprocate those feelings.
The song's poignant melody and haunting instrumentation create a sense of melancholy and yearning, drawing listeners into the emotional journey of heartbreak and acceptance.
