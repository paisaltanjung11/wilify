Tired of Wanting You

"Tired of Wanting You" by Boy with Uke is a soulful ballad that delves into the complex emotions of unrequited love.
With gentle strumming and heartfelt vocals, the song captures the bittersweet essence of longing for someone who
remains out of reach. Each verse is a poignant reflection of the internal struggle, as the protagonist grapples with the
pain of desire and the realization that they must find the strength to let go.
