Car’s Outside

"Cars Outside" by James is an evocative exploration of longing and introspection set against a backdrop of nighttime solitude.
With its haunting melodies and soulful vocals, the song paints a vivid picture of a solitary figure gazing out at the world from the
confines of their room, watching the cars pass by outside. The lyrics delve into themes of isolation, yearning, and the desire for connection,
as the protagonist grapples with feelings of loneliness and the search for meaning in the midst of urban life.
