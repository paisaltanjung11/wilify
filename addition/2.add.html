Daylight

"Daylight" by David is a poignant and introspective ballad that delves into themes of self-discovery, resilience, and hope.
With its heartfelt lyrics and emotive vocals, the song captures the journey of overcoming obstacles and finding the strength to embrace new beginnings.
The melody is uplifting yet contemplative, evoking a sense of longing and reflection.
As the song progresses, it builds into a powerful anthem of empowerment, inspiring listeners to find solace in the promise of a brighter tomorrow.
