Willify Music Streaming Platform
This repository contains the prototype and implementation of the Willify website, a music streaming platform designed to offer an engaging and seamless experience for music enthusiasts. 
The project encompasses five main pages: Home, Register, Songs, About Us, and Song Detail, each crafted with a focus on user experience and responsive design.

Features
Home Page: Showcases best-selling and recommended songs along with a brief introduction to Willify.
Songs Page: Displays a comprehensive list of songs, including names, descriptions, and images to aid customer decision-making.
Register Page: Contains a registration form requiring personal information such as name, email, password, age, and gender. The form includes five types of components and validation using JavaScript.
About Us Page: Provides information about Willify, including its history and founders.
Song Detail Page: Displays detailed information about each available song, including its name, description, and lyrics.

Technology Stack
HTML: Structure of the website.
CSS: External styles for layout and design.
JavaScript: Interactivity and form validation.
Figma: Prototyping tool for designing the website layout and user interface.
